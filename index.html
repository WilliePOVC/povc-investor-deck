Overview

Opportunity
Summary

Press On Ventures is a $20 million early-stage consumer venture fund investing in the next generation of transformative consumer companies.

Fund Size

$20 Million Early-Stage Consumer Generalist Venture Capital Fund

Investment Size

$250,000 to $500,000 per initial check

Investment Strategy

  • Investing from Pre-Seed through Series A
  • Targeting 2% to 6% Ownership
  • Partner with 20 to 25 Exceptional Founders
  • 80/20 Split Between Consumer Technology and Consumer Products

Fees & Structure

  • 5 Year Initial Investment Period; 10 Year Full Fund Term
  • 15% Capital Reserved for Follow-On Investments
  • Bi-Annual Capital Calls
  • 2.5% Management Fee (reduced 0.25%/yr after investment period, floor 1.5%)
  • 20% Carried Interest

10Beauty

Automated Manicure Technology

$1.5M Invested 2.08% Ownership
  • Operates the world’s first fully automated, salon-quality manicure machine, using a proprietary 7-camera computer vision system and robotic arm to deliver an end-to-end manicure without human intervention.
  • Co-founded by the team behind ALICE (acquired by Expedia for $130M) plus an original Roomba engineer; raised $50M+ with signed commitments for ~1,000 locations and live pilot deployments inside Ulta Beauty.
Founders: Alex Shashou & Justin Effron

Cofertility

Free Egg Freezing via Donation

$460K Invested 1% Ownership
  • Reimagines fertility through a human-centered platform whose flagship “Split” program lets women freeze their eggs for free in exchange for donating half to intended parents who can’t otherwise conceive.
  • Tackles a $10K–$20K affordability gap in a category dominated by opaque incumbents; has raised ~$20M, matched donors and intended parents in the quadruple digits, and built a uniquely diverse donor pool (>50% women of color) that incumbents can’t replicate.
Founders: Lauren Makler & Halle Tecco
Press On Ventures Deal Memo →

Feno

Smart Toothbrush & Oral Health

$250K Invested 0.70% Ownership
  • AI-powered U-shaped Smartbrush that cleans all teeth simultaneously in 20 seconds, with an embedded oral scanner and companion app that turns daily brushing into dentist-grade health monitoring.
  • Sits at the intersection of two large, durable markets: the multi-billion-dollar electric oral care category and connected-health/preventive-care, with a defensible AI moat built on a proprietary database of 20,000+ oral scans.
Founders: Hamet Watt & Kenny Brown
Press On Ventures Deal Memo →

Jacob Bar

The Healthiest Protein Bar

$500K Invested 5% Ownership
  • A clean-label, high-protein bar with 20g of grass-fed whey, beef tallow, organic honey and dates, and zero seed oils or artificial additives engineered for whole-foods nutrition consumers.
  • Differentiated in a crowded protein bar category by riding the cultural shift away from seed oils and ultra-processed ingredients, with a clean ingredient deck that resonates with the same audience driving the rise of brands like RXBAR, David, and Chomps.
Founders: Jake Levy
Press On Ventures Deal Memo →

Rhythm Science

AI Cardiac Care Platform

$300K Invested 1% Ownership
  • Cloud-based virtual cardiac care platform that aggregates data from implantable and wearable devices to enable remote monitoring and management of hypertension, heart failure, and rhythm disorders, with AI-assisted triage.
  • Goes after one of the highest-cost, highest-volume problems in U.S. healthcare (cardiovascular disease is the #1 cause of death), with a software-led model aligned to reimbursement tailwinds in remote patient monitoring and value-based care.
Founders: Shawn Kumar

Premium Chocolate-Covered Dates

$300K Invested 5% Ownership

  • Rides the same “better-for-you indulgence” wave fueling brands like Mid-Day Squares and Hu Chocolate, but uniquely positioned as a luxurious, giftable treat with strong social-driven momentum on Instagram and TikTok and a growing footprint of retail locations.

Magic Story

$300K Invested 1% Ownership

  • Founded by former Sony Pictures filmmakers already serving 100,000+ families with a 4.9-star rating; combines a recurring monthly subscription (Magic Story+) with high-margin gifting to build a defensible IP and content flywheel.

Recess

AI Parenting Platform

$400K Invested 5% Ownership
  • A modern online community where parents connect, learn from each other, and buy or sell gently used baby gear in a trusted, parent-vetted marketplace.
  • Targets the massive parenting commerce category by combining community and resale, two of the strongest engagement and retention drivers in consumer, at a moment when secondhand and circular consumption are becoming the default for millennial and Gen Z parents.
Founders: Ethan Arpi & Swati Vauthrin

SipMargs

Premium RTD Margaritas

$538K Invested 4.33% Ownership
  • A premium ready-to-drink margarita in a can, made with real tequila and 100% natural ingredients. A no-mix, no-compromise cocktail experience.
  • Plays directly into the two biggest tailwinds in beverage alcohol: the explosive growth of RTD cocktails and the continued premiumization of tequila, with a clean-ingredient story that differentiates it from sugary, additive-heavy incumbents.
Founders: Justin Nabozna & Alix Earle

Skylark

Luxury Travel Remnant Inventory

$300K Invested 5% Ownership
  • A modern luxury travel platform that lets travelers self-book top-tier hotels and flights with optional human advisor support, VIP perks, and exclusive rates — blending the convenience of OTAs with the access of a traditional travel agent.
  • Captures the fastest-growing slice of travel (luxury and premium leisure) with a tech-forward, hybrid model that leverages preferred-partner GDS economics.
Founders: Miles McMullin & Mike Greene

Property Maintenance Platform

Vuelo

AI-Native Travel & BNPL Platform

$685K Invested 2% Ownership
  • The UK’s first AI-native travel platform, replacing the traditional search bar with a proprietary AI engine that embeds a personalized payment plan into every trip recommendation—customers see “Barcelona, 5 nights, £74/month” instead of a sticker price.
  • Founded by Jasper Dykes (ex-CEO, Fly Now Pay Later), FCA-authorized, and already onboarding thousands of new travelers each month. Raised £56M in seed (Backed VC, Play Ventures, Viola Credit) to capture the most underserved slice of a BNPL category projected to grow to $580B by 2030.
